## English (en-US)

### App name (max 30 chars)
KanaRead — Hiragana & Katakana

### Short description (max 80 chars)
Read real Japanese words. Practice hiragana and katakana, offline and ad-free.

### Full description (max 4000 chars)
KanaRead is a clean, distraction-free way to learn to *read* hiragana and katakana — the two basic Japanese alphabets — by practicing on real words.

Instead of drilling isolated symbols, KanaRead shows you a word written in kana and asks you to type its reading in romaji. You build kana fluency the same way children do: by reading actual words, getting instant feedback, and slowly speeding up.

WHAT YOU CAN DO
• Practice hiragana, katakana, or both mixed together.
• Type the romaji reading and check your answer instantly.
• Track your best score, average response time, and progress per mode.
• Switch between light and dark themes.
• Pick up where you left off — your records are saved on your device.

WHY KANAREAD
• 100% offline — no internet, no account, no sign-up.
• No ads, no tracking, no personal data collected.
• Lightweight: under a few megabytes, fast on any modern Android device.
• Modern Material You design that adapts to your system theme.
• Built and maintained as a personal project — feedback always welcome.

WHO IT'S FOR
• Beginners who already know a few kana and want to read words confidently.
• Self-learners who don't want yet another social/gamified language app.
• Anyone preparing for JLPT N5 / N4 reading practice.
• Travellers wanting to read Japanese signs, menus, and product labels.

KanaRead does not teach grammar or vocabulary meaning. It focuses on one thing — recognising kana fast — and tries to do that one thing well.

PRIVACY-FIRST
KanaRead works completely offline. There is no analytics, no ads, no cloud sync. Your scores stay on your phone.

## Slovak (sk-SK)

KanaRead je jednoduchá a prehľadná aplikácia na učenie *čítania* hiragany a katakany — dvoch základných japonských abecied — pomocou skutočných slov.

Namiesto driloania jednotlivých znakov ti KanaRead ukáže celé slovo napísané v kane a ty napíšeš jeho výslovnosť v rómadži. Postupne získaš plynulosť čítania rovnako, ako sa to učia japonské deti — čítaním reálnych slov, s okamžitou spätnou väzbou a postupným zrýchľovaním.

ČO PONÚKA
• Tréning hiragany, katakany alebo oboch dohromady.
• Napíš výslovnosť (rómadži) a aplikácia ti hneď povie, či je správna.
• Sleduj si najlepšie skóre, priemerný čas odpovede a pokrok podľa režimu.
• Svetlá aj tmavá téma.
• Pokračuj tam, kde si skončil — záznamy sú uložené v zariadení.

PREČO KANAREAD
• 100 % offline — žiadny internet, žiadne konto, žiadna registrácia.
• Bez reklám, bez sledovania, bez zbierania osobných údajov.
